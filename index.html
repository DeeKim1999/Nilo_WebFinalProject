🏱   WELCOME ABOARD THE SCHOLAR STATION!   🏱

You've arrived at Scholar Station – all aboard for a scholarly journey! Click on different areas of our train map below to explore:

Choose wisely or enjoy the scenic route!

Not ready to choose a destination? No worries, take your time to enjoy the view! Let us introduce the Scholar Station:

At Scholar Station, we're your one-stop shop for books and school supplies. Whether you're diving into literature or gearing up for class, we've got you covered!

Once upon a time in a small town nestled in Agno, Pangasinan, a vibrant idea was born out of a simple yet significant need. Students here yearned for affordable school supplies and books but faced the challenge of traveling nearly an hour to the nearest city to fulfill these basic needs. The lack of accessible resources sparked a desire to create a solution that would benefit the entire community. Thus, Jovy's Scholar Station was envisioned as a local community hub for educational essentials, discussions, and inspiration.


Determined to bridge this gap, Jovy's Scholar Station came into being. With a passion for education and a commitment to accessibility, we set up shop in the heart of Agno. Our humble store quickly became a beacon of hope for students, offering not only books and school supplies but also a sense of community and possibility. As our bookstore grew, so did our mission to empower young minds through knowledge and creativity. We strive to foster a love for learning among students of all ages. We aim to extend far and wide to more remote areas.
